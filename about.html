Our Objectives

  • To encourage MMU students to get involved in table tennis and promote leisure and relaxation beyond academics.
  • To develop MMU students' table tennis skills, fostering a learning and growth environment that encourages personal development and confidence.
  • Organizing tournaments and matches, both within the club and against external institutions, to foster a spirit of healthy competition and encourage good sportsmanship.
  • To establish the MMU Table Tennis team as one of the well-known and respected teams feared by other universities.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission: Create a space for skill development, sportsmanship, and friendships through table tennis.

Vision: Be recognized as the top university table tennis club in Malaysia.
