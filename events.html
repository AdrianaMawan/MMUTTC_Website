Past Events

We take pride in organizing events that bring our members together and foster a competitive spirit. Here are some of our notable past activities:

  • Sportsmania Day: A day filled with friendly matches and fun activities to engage members and the MMU community.
  • Members’ Day: Celebrating our club members with team-building games and appreciation ceremonies.
  • Inter-University Friendly Matches: Conducted in June 2024, fostering camaraderie and competition among universities.
  • Training Sessions: Regularly scheduled training sessions to help members improve their skills and techniques.
  • Annual General Meeting 2025: Held on 14th January 2025 where students can learn more about the club as well as get to know the upcoming commitee lineup.

Achievements

Our club is proud to have achieved notable successes in various tournaments and events:

  • 4th Place: MASISWA Zon Tengah Tournament 2023.
  • Silver Medal (Women’s Category): SUKIPT 2024.
  • 3rd Place: Table Tennis Tournament at Xiamen University Malaysia.
